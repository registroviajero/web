Today we officially launch **RegistroViajero**, a platform built to help tourist accommodation agencies in Spain comply with Royal Decree 933/2021 without the headaches.

## Why we built RegistroViajero

Since December 2024, every tourist accommodation in Spain has been required to send guest data to the Spanish Ministry of the Interior electronically. The process is cumbersome: complex forms, specific formats, and a system (SES.HOSPEDAJES) that is not exactly intuitive.

We built RegistroViajero so you can focus on what matters — taking care of your guests — while we handle the paperwork.

## What you can do with RegistroViajero

- **Digital check-in**: send a link to each guest so they can fill in their details from their phone, in their own language.
- **Automatic reservation import**: connect your calendars from Booking.com, Airbnb, VRBO, and more.
- **Direct submission to the Ministry**: generate and send guest reports to SES.HOSPEDAJES with one click.
- **Team management**: invite your team with distinct roles and permissions.

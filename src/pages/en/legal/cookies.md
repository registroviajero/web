# Cookies Policy

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store in your browser to remember information about your visit.

## Cookies and local storage

RegistroViajero.com **does not use cookies** for analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.

The service uses the browser's local storage (localStorage) for:

| Key | Purpose | Duration |
|-----|---------|----------|
| token | Authentication session (JWT) | Until session logout |
| locale | Language preference | Permanent |

## Web analytics

We use [Umami](https://umami.is), a privacy-friendly web analytics tool. Umami does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and complies with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation, EU Regulation 2016/679) without the need for consent. Metrics are aggregated and anonymous.

## Managing storage

You can delete local storage data from your browser settings. Note that if you delete it, you will need to log in again.

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/es/kb/cookies-informacion-que-los-sitios-web-guardan-en-)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/es-es/guide/safari/sfri11471/)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/es-es/microsoft-edge/eliminar-las-cookies-en-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

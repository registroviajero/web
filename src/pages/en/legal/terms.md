# Terms of Use

## 1. Definitions

- **RegistroViajero** / **Platform**: the SaaS service accessible at app.registroviajero.com.
- **User**: person who creates an account to manage a tourist accommodation agency.
- **Guest**: person whose data is collected through a check-in link.
- **Agency**: the entity or natural person that owns the accommodations managed on the platform.

## 2. Description of the service

RegistroViajero enables:
- The collection of traveler data through individual check-in links.
- The management of lodging reports ("partes de hospedaje") and bookings.
- The submission of communications to the Spanish Ministry of the Interior through SES.HOSPEDAJES.
- The generation of PDF documents with the data from the records.

## 3. Plans and pricing

| Plan | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Trial | €0 (15 days) | Unlimited accommodations during the trial, unlimited guests, submission to the Ministry |
| Pro | €5/active accommodation/month | Pay per active accommodation (no minimum), unlimited records, CSV/PDF export, priority support |

Only accommodations active during the period are billed; adding or removing accommodations is prorated. Prices may be updated with at least 30 days' prior notice by email. After the trial period ends, the Pro plan must be subscribed to in order to continue submitting reports to the Ministry.
